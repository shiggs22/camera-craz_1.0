ESO 5D Description
  • Newly designed 22.3 Megapixel full-frame CMOS sensor, 14-bit A/D conversion, wide range ISO setting 100-25600 for shooting from bright to dim light and next generation DIGIC 5+ Image Processor for enhanced noise reduction and exceptional processing speed

  • EOS HD Video with manual exposure control, multiple frame rates and selectable All i-frame or IPB compression
